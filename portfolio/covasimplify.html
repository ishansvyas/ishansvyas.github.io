CovaSimplify: An Agent-Based Epidemic Model Accelerator

Accelerating Epidemic Simulation using Custom Hardware

Project Overview

During disease outbreaks, people like policymakers or hospital staff need to know when a disease is likely to exponentially increase in frequency. Simulations and models can offer a glimpse into when they might occur, but there are different types of models, each with their own benefits and drawbacks. A specific type of model, called an Agent-Based Model, is particularly accurate and easy to use, but is computationally intense. For that reason, we converted a popular Agent-Based Epidemic Model into a hardware accelerator called CovaSimplify.
